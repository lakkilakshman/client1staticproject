About us

Guntur is a small city (on the Eastern side of Hyderabad), just like a twin city to Vijayawada, situated on the other side of Krishna river. Guntur has grown fast as far as professional institutes are concerned. It is, in fact, the largest and a well developed city in A.P. The most famous places in Guntur include Manasa Sarovar, Haailand which is situated on Guntur-Vijayawada highway, Kondaveedu Fort, the Undavalli caves, Amaravati, Uppalapadu Gardens and Prakasam Barrage. Guntur has a number of tourist destinations on the outskirts of the city too! They are well connected by bus or you can just drive down and visit temples and museums. Krishna Barrage or Prakasam Barrage is located 30 kms. from Guntur. You can visit Nagarjunasagar dam and also see the enthralling caves in Undavalli on the banks of river Krishna.
